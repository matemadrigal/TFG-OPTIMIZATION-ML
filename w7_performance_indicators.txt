A Realistic Backtest
Backtesting

     Realistic Backtests
         · Backtests should include all realiastic assumptions
         · Historic costing or costing model (i.e., mid, bid-ask spread,
           market impact measure) for market orders
         · Elements like roll and roll cost (i.e., when rolling from ERZ3 to
           ERH4 when we come to the Dec IMM date, we usually pay 1/2 *
           bid-ask spread for the two trades)
         · Financing if bought on a margin, including long and/or short
           side for most costly version)
         · Can summarize returns before and after costs, etc.

Multi-Objective Optimization
Multiple Objectives

     Multiple Objectives
         · Many design problems involve a large number of competing
           objectives
         · These are common in areas of self-driving vehicles,
           search-engine optimization, and many other areas
         · Technically MOO is at play in mean-variance optimization in
           finance: we want to maximize returns while minimizing risk.
         · Trying to do both simultaneously leads to what is known as a
           Pareto solution - which gives us the efficient frontier

Multiple Objectives

     Multiple Objectives- Linear Combinations
         · MOO works for up to 4 objective functions
         · MOO is equivalent to having linear combinations, i.e.,
                            max f1(x) + 2f2(x) + 3f3(x) + · · · nfn(x)
         · Having more than four objectives  Many-Objective
           Optimization (MaOO)
         · Some methods for MaOO include finding PCAs of objectives and
           then using Pareto-methods on the principal components
         · Once the parameter space is explored, a single choice must be
           done!
